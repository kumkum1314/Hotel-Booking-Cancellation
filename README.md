# 🏨 Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis

## 📌 Project Summary

This project analyzes hotel booking data to uncover key factors driving booking cancellations and provide actionable insights to improve revenue and customer retention. The analysis focuses on identifying patterns across customer segments, booking channels, and seasonal trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

High booking cancellation rates directly impact hotel revenue and operational planning.
This project aims to:

* Identify **key drivers of cancellations**
* Understand **customer behavior patterns**
* Suggest **data-driven strategies to reduce cancellations**

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Dashboard Development

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

* Overall **cancellation rate analysis**
* Segment-wise comparison (customer type, booking type)
* Impact of **booking channels** on cancellations
* **Seasonal trends** and booking patterns
* Lead time vs cancellation relationship

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📌 **Online bookings** showed significantly higher cancellation rates compared to offline channels
* 📌 Customers with **longer lead time** were more likely to cancel
* 📌 Certain customer segments (e.g., transient customers) had higher cancellation frequency
* 📌 Seasonal trends indicated **peak cancellations during specific months**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Implement **stricter cancellation policies** for high-risk booking channels
* Offer **incentives for non-refundable or early confirmation bookings**
* Improve targeting of **high-value, low-cancellation customer segments**
* Optimize pricing and availability during high-risk cancellation periods
